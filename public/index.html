CREATING THE FIRST DIGITAL HORA

Last year 1dece.ro wanted to “move the capital of Romania” and this year's goal is to create the first and largest digital hora.

HORA you say?

Yes, hora is a traditional Romanian folk dance where the dancers hold each other's hands forming a circle. The dance is usually accompanied by musical instruments.

Why? Because for this year’s National Day of Romania we want to give a little feeling of what is truly 100% Romanian. The heritage, tradition, folk. Now get your click to work and join the dance!
